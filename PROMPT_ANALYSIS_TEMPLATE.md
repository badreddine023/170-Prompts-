## üîß **QUICK OPTIMIZATION FORMULAS**

### Basic Prompt Structure
```
[ROLE]: You are a [specific expert role] with [specific expertise].

[CONTEXT]: [Relevant background information]

[TASK]: [Clear, specific instruction]

[FORMAT]: Provide your response as [specific format].

[CONSTRAINTS]: 
- Constraint 1
- Constraint 2
```

### Enhanced Structure with Chain-of-Thought
```
[ROLE]: You are a [specific expert role].

[CONTEXT]: [Background information]

[TASK]: [Main instruction]

[PROCESS]: 
1. First, [step 1]
2. Then, [step 2]
3. Finally, [step 3]

[FORMAT]: Structure your response as:
- Section 1: [description]
- Section 2: [description]

[CONSTRAINTS]:
- [Constraint 1]
- [Constraint 2]
```
